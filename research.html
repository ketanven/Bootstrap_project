RESEARCH

Research Studies

Research will be one of the most promoted and motivated areas by edustage University among the students. Research will be the fundamental essence of the University, and to uphold the spirit, the University will provide opportunities for research in various disciplines. To motivate the students to go beyond conventional research, the University will offer various grants and sponsorships which will facilitate the students to make research boundaries limitless.

Courses Offered

Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.)

Benefits

Undertaking a doctoral research degree has many benefits. It gives the research scholar anopportunity to:
Make an original, distinct contribution to knowledge
Learn to communicate research findings effectively within the academia, to industry and internationally
Collaborate with world-class, leading experts in the chosen field
Develop key transferable skills highly sought by employers
Grow personally and professionally
Join the community of scholars in the discipline of research
Comprehensive student support networks.

Doctoral Programme Ph.D

edustage University offers its doctoral programme designed as per International Standards. Research scholars will be encouraged to work with international subject experts to understand the global scenarios for their research area.
The University will promote innovation and excellence in research education and researcher development across the University. It will provide a range of services to support and develop the research students, supervisors and early career researchers including research education programmes, policy development, advice and guidance and scholarships.

The areas available for Ph.D. are:

  • Business and Management
  • Commerce
  • Information Technology and Computer Sciences
  • Design
  • Law
  • Interdisciplinary

    CENTER FOR RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT

    Preamble:

    Research is an integral component of higher education. Acknowledging this fact, Gujarat Law Society, the sponsoring body of edustage University, established the edustage Centre for Research and Development (edustageCRD). This was prior to the establishment of edustage University. With the coming into being of the edustage University, edustageCRD has become a part of the University. edustage CRD has taken several initiatives to promote research and providing training facilities to the faculty members of the University. This has helped improve the research profile of the University.
